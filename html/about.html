    My name is Henry Nguyen and I am a teenager who's very passionate about software engineering.

    Where are you from?

    What do you enjoy the most about software development?

    I enjoy the problem solving and piecing-together aspects of programming the most because they challenge me to be creative. I used to hate those parts of programming the most but through developing my skills and knowledge, I now embrace them as fun challenges.

    Why did you get into software engineering?

    I originally got into software engineering because I wanted to a career where I can have location and time freedom while also making a lot of money. Now, I would say that I just love coding because I am aware of its immense potential to benefit humanity.

    How do you stay motivated to continue programming?

    I stay motivated by finding new things to improve on and since there's so much to learn in the programming world, I have an endless amount of learning and improving to do.
