About

    Who are you?

    My name is Henry Nguyen, and I am a teenager who's very passionate about web development and software engineering.

    Where are you from?

    I was born in Silver Springs, Maryland but raised in Pealand, Texas.

    What do you enjoy the most about web development?

    I enjoy the problem solving and piecing-together aspects of programming the most because they challenge me to be creative.

    I used to hate those parts of programming the most but through developing my skills and knowledge, I now embrace them as learning experiences.

    Why did you get into web development?

    I originally got into software engineering because I wanted to a career where I can have location and time freedom while also making a lot of money.

    Now, I would say that I just love coding because I want to contribute to something bigger than myself through web development.

    How do you stay motivated to continue programming?

    I stay motivated by finding new things to improve on and since there's so much to learn in the programming world, I have an endless amount of learning and improving to do.
