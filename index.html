About Me

Whether running 10 miles every week, getting rag-dolled by black belts in jiu-jitsu, or creating my own APIs, there has always been a masochistic desire inside me to challenge myself and improve. Among STEM subjects, I have always found computer science the most difficult and fascinating. Despite how much computers have shaped our modern world, curiosity kills me with the thought of how they work. How am I able to communicate with strangers across the world on Instagram by typing on my phone? How am I able to watch all these movies and shows on Netflix from the comfort of my own home?

This fascination led me to programming, specifically web development. During my junior and senior years, I studied HTML, CSS, and JavaScript after school through a bootcamp. Most of my learning occurred through creating projects such as websites and databases. It was so rewarding to see a project come to life after weeks of mental gymnastics and programming purgatory. Now as a fully-fledged adult, I intend to bring my useful skillsets to the workplace to help companies build and maintain websites. An urge for more experience and challenge leads me towards a path of greater responsibility and productivity, allowing me to help brands and individuals achieve recognition.
